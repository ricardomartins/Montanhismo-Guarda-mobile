# Privacy Policy - Clube de Montanhismo da Guarda (Mobile)

Last updated: **March 29, 2026**

[Ricardo Martins](https://github.com/ricardomartins) built the ClubeMG Mobile app as an [Open-Source app](https://github.com/ricardomartins/Montanhismo-Guarda-mobile). This SERVICE is provided by *Clube de Montanhismo da Guarda* at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

1. **Information Collection and Use:** The ClubeMG Mobile app was designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII) on our servers.
    - **Bookmarks:** When you save an event as a bookmark, that information is saved locally on your own device.
    - **Internet Access:** The application requires an internet connection to download the list of events, calendars, and images associated with them.
    - **Notifications Permission:** The application requests the notifications permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) exclusively to alert you about events you have bookmarked. No information about your usage habits is sent outwards.
    
2. **Data Sharing with Third Parties:** Since we do not collect personal data, we also do not share, sell, or rent any type of personal information to third parties.
3. **Security:** We value your trust in using our application. The data generated by the app (such as your bookmark preferences) is retained in the secure storage of your device's Android operating system.
4. **Links to Other Sites:** The application may contain links to other sites (for example, on the "Visit Page" button for events). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites.
5. **Changes to This Privacy Policy:** We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
6. **Contact Us:** If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:
    - Email: clube@montanhismo-guarda.pt
    - Website: https://ricardomartins.github.io/Montanhismo-Guarda-mobile/
